About FoodShare

Our Mission

FoodShare was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between food surplus and food scarcity. Every day, tons of perfectly good food goes to waste while millions go hungry. We're changing that narrative by connecting those with extra food to those who need it most.

What We Do

We've built a technology platform that connects food donors (restaurants, events, households) directly with verified NGOs and community organizations. Our network of delivery volunteers ensures that surplus food reaches those who need it most, quickly and efficiently.

Our Values

  • Community First: Building connections that strengthen communities
  • Zero Waste: Every plate of food matters
  • Transparency: Open, honest operations you can trust
  • Dignity: Treating everyone with respect and compassion

How FoodShare Works

1

For Donors

Sign up and create a donation listing with details about the food (type, quantity, pickup location, availability time). Upload photos if possible. Nearby NGOs get notified automatically.

2

For NGOs

Register and get verified by our admin team. Browse available donations in your area. Accept donations that match your needs. Request pickup or arrange your own collection.

3

For Delivery Volunteers

Sign up as a delivery volunteer. Get assigned pickup and delivery tasks. Use our app to navigate routes efficiently. Update status in real-time.

The Complete Journey

  1. Donor posts available food with all details
  2. System notifies nearby verified NGOs
  3. NGO reviews and accepts the donation
  4. Admin assigns delivery personnel (if needed)
  5. Delivery person picks up food from donor
  6. Food is delivered to NGO safely
  7. Both parties confirm delivery completion
  8. Impact is tracked and reported
